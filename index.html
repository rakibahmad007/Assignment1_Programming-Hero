The Vision of Future

The vision for Bangladesh 2.0 in technology is to build a digital economy and knowledge-based society. The government's goal is to use technology to improve services, increase transparency, and empower citizens.

Inventions

Inventions are the products of human creativity and ingenuity. They are the tools that help us solve problems and make our lives easier. In Bangladesh 2.0, we are working on new inventions that will revolutionize the way we live and work.
